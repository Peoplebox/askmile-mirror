10 Food Couples Should Try Together

Team Askmile.com–Happiness, Relationship–March 24, 2018March 29, 2018

We’ve all heard that a way to someone’s heart is via their stomach. Here are the top 10 CUPID-APPROVED food that will spark the flame. Tuck in with your date and let the magic unfold. Mind you, ‘the tastier the food, the naughtier the date scenes get’.

1. SPAGHETTI       

Spaghetti is the perfect metaphor for LOVE. The long noodles: “A symbol of the longevity of your pairing”. Have you ever seen Lady and the Tramp? There’s nothing wrong with taking a cue from it. There’s no way you have never wanted to try that with your partner!

2. WINE

“Wine is the ultimate expression of love”. Seriously, what else is more romantic than wine? Obviously, you and your darling! It’s the elixir of lovers. So, why wait? Pour a glass of wine for you and your sweetheart.  And look how romantic your date gets….. well, I’d say “Wine, dine and go for the night”., you know what I mean!

3. CHAAT

You’re probably wondering what wonders can golgappa or paapad chaat do on your date. Well trust me, nothing can compare to the happiness of hogging and gulping down as many as you can, and competing with your partner at it., Masal poori, pav bhaji, bedmi, pakora, kachori or be it any other chaat., will definitely throw up cravings and nostalgia during your date! Don’t hold back, feed each other. You’re allowed tonight.

4. CHOCOLATE FONDUE


What an awesome way to reconnect with your date! Wine by candle light with fruit covered in rich dark chocolate?! There’s always something so classy & romantic about chocolate and a bubbly sparkling wine. Fondue is such a special treat, that it makes a great date idea! Honestly, if you trust someone not to double-dip..or don’t care if they do, that’s love…

5. LASAGNA

“ALL YOU NEED IS LOVE & LASAGNA”

Looking for something as luscious as you and your partner? Well, then lasagna has got your back. Place the lasagna between you and your significant other…crack open the gooey centre open together. It may run a little. Sexy. Perfect for your sexy night-out!

6. PANEER TIKKA

You’re going to have a perfect start with these perfect starters! Well, if your date is a ‘pure non-vegan’, then don’t worry.., go for sheek kebab! To wax poetic: view the assortment as the various elements of your relationship…the toppings: a unique mix of quirks you each bring and the dip: the love you have for each other, infinite!

7. BUBBLE TEA


Looking to up the fun-factor in your romance? The bubble tea is guaranteed to have you and your S.O cheered up for the perfect and fun-filled romantic date. Make sure your partner is watching as you tilt your head back, close your eyes and slurp back the tender tapioca pearls.

8. CHICKEN STROGANOFF

Slow cooked meat, overflowing with velvety, hot curry will ignite the carnal cravings. The whole experience is animalistic and elegant all at once. This dish is the perfect mantra for a wild night!

9. WAFFLES

There’s something sweeter than your relationship.., yeah you guessed it right, ‘WAFFLES’. The romantic gesture-circus waffles make the date romantic and fuel the flames of love. The mood of the night is guaranteed to be relaxed and playful. And speaking of playful isn’t this a great food for a unique date?!

10. FRIED FISH WITH POTATO FRIES


As if the suggestive name of the dish wasn’t enough to set the mood, the dish’s contrast is guaranteed to win hearts, just as how you and your partner have contrasted each other and made a perfect duo!

However, IF YOU’RE LOOKING FOR LOVE, MIGHT AS WELL GIVE THESE MEALS A SHOT. YOU’VE GOT NOTHING TO LOSE!

“COUPLES WHO EAT TOGETHER, STAY TOGETHER”
