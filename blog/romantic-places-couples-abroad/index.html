Top 10 romantic places to visit for couples in abroad

aafiya–Relationship, travelling with spouse, Uncategorized–April 17, 2018April 19, 2018

All the honey-mooners, newly engaged, hopeless romantics, typical going to surprise ‘bae’ lovers, here we bring to you the utopia of romantic places across the world. These 10 exotic places to visit are guaranteed to win your hearts. You can thank us later! For now, start the exploration.

[Also read: How travelling helps couples bond together]

1.MALDIVES

Just imagine you and your darling alone in an island and going deep sea diving with the dolphins and manta rays! Honestly, there could be no better ways to get romantic than this. After all the diving and riding you could go for an erotic gourmet dinner at a sandbank in the middle of the ocean. In short, a trip to Maldives is a trip to the ‘cloud nine’ and we certainly do know that you are going to deny it saying,”Gawd, it’s a trip to the heavens”.

 

 

2.PARIS

 

Ah, how could we forget the ‘city of love’, when listing the romantic places to visit?! A cruise on the Seine, overlooking the Eiffel tower and kissing your bae is just the perfect metaphor of LOVE. From the strolls across Champs-Élysées to the French kisses at Pont des Arts, Paris has got all the magical spells to make anyone fall in love with it. Très  romantique!

 

3.Switzerland

Switzerland does look like a place from the Disney’s, doesn’t it? From lush green grasslands to the snow-capped mountain to sleighing in the sledges to skiing in the mountains, it has got all the ingredients to the potion of love, it may seem fictional, but who knows you might end up turning fictional characters in this magical place.

http://traveltriangle.com/blog/honeymoon-places-in-switzerland/

 

 

4. SANTORINI

When your love is picture perfect and Santorini decides to embrace it, with it’s picturesque landscape. The trademark white Cycladic houses amidst the deep blue waters is a heart throb location, that’ll set the mood right. A candle light dinner in this ‘out of the world’ place will bring out the Romeo & Juliet in you.

5. AMSTERDAM

*Boom boom boom clap*. We know exactly what you’re thinking of right now! THE FAULT IN OUR STARS. There could be no one who wouldn’t want to visit Amsterdam after watching TFIOS, and even if you haven’t watched, you’ll definitely want to visit this vintage architectural scenic place with it’s ultimate sophistication. And isn’t this more than enough to visit Amsterdam?

6. FIJI

The Taveuni- a cigar shaped island, a massive shield volcano which rises from the floor of the pacific ocean is a unique and a romatic place to visit. From rainbow-reef snorkelling to Lavena coastal walk, this place has so many more unique surprises in stock for you! And we too have a surprise for you! You need no visa to visit Fiji!

 

 

 

7. SRI LANKA

 

For the couples who want to stay low-key and have a downtown experience, Sri Lanka has got your back! The culture, gastronomy and the ambiance will sync with your perfect romantic getaway. Sri Lanka lacks nothing when it comes to a romantic destination.

 

 

 8.TUSCANY

 

Nothing is more romantic than being treated like royalty and Tuscany is all you need to be felt like one! The definition of a romantic destination is Tuscany. This place isn’t all extravaganzas and lavish, it’s a ‘makes-us-feel-good’ place and isn’t this more than what you want in a romantic getaway with your prince charming or the belle princess?

9. CARMEL, CALIFORNIA

We’re not sure if it’s love at first sight for you and your S.O, but it’s definitely going to be love at first sight when you visit this place…Well, we’re guessing you’re already in love with this place. This small village in California is a dream destination for countless couples. One could find no more heavenly place than this. A walk through the dense groves of Cypress forest leading to hidden coves & beaches is definitely “A WALK TO REMEMBER”

 

10. SAVANNAH


You+ your better half + Savannah= Love. Oh, what a way to get lost with your love on this planet? This oldest city of Georgia is truly a nature lover’s delight, from the grand trees to the subtle beach, love is in the air. An epitome of architecture, this place has got all the tools to craft your love into a perfect masterpiece.

Why you should visit these romantic places with your better-half?

According to a survey of 1,000 couples about understand how important traveling affected their relationship positively, almost two thirds (63%) of the respondents claimed that traveling has helped them stay together. The truth is not so far-fetched, when you travel together, there are sudden and exciting elements that fire your relationship. Couples share a common goal and purpose to see the world together. The anticipation and yearning of unraveling destinations, committing themselves to an endless adventure somehow bonds such couples and offers them a reason to always want to be together.

 

Traveling is revealing, not only to the outer world but also to each other. Couples discover their strengths and weaknesses and find out how to complement each on these roles as they keep on uncovering paths and destinations. According to a survey it was found out that couples who travel together agree better and have fewer disagreements than couples who don’t travel together. Traveling makes them more understanding and patient for each other. According to a survey, couples who travel together have a better sexual relationship than couples who don’t travel together. Traveling together cuts the work and stress into half, enough to spark romance and affection. According to the survey more than three quarters of those who traveled admitted that they have a good sex life. The indelible treasure engraved in your hearts and minds when experiencing something together can be everlasting. What is new sort of creates a memory that will be forever unique to their relationships.

Travelling together boosts your relationship

Since you are together most of the time, there is a little space for keeping ugly secrets. There are no facades and you have to appreciate your partner the way he or she is. Whether he snores, has his underarm hair un-shaved or has her legs un-shaved, traveling reveals a bunch of ugly truths. And with this there is little or nothing to hide. You can’t travel together without turning up some humor here and there. There are times when things just go horribly wrong and you have to laugh over it. It could be the horrible food you just bought on the street corner, having your hotel room mixed up or losing a map… something goes wrong and it is enough to have something to laugh at and sustain your happiness along the way. It goes beyond what you watch on cinema screens or read in any book, as a traveling couple you live the romance out of spontaneity and a state of mind that you could be led anywhere and something could sparkle another moment of beauty. It is never about the money but the experience that traveling together could bring. This is why 86% of respondents in a survey of traveling couples said that their relationship still had romance alive in it, compared to 73% of respondents who never traveled together. While other couples who don’t travel together worry about the future, and build their relationship with doubts, couples who traveled together were not worried about what was going to happen next because they were consumed and captured by the intensity of the wonderful moments experienced with each other.

 

You get to know each other better

Traveling together didn’t give them the opportunity to over-analyze their situation and be critical on several subjects but made them appreciate the present moment Since they have only each other to turn to and fewer disruptions by external bodies or persons they were able to give themselves their all to become better friends. They would stick with each other through challenges and differences to provide each other with the companion they need.They are educated together. Traveling offers an opportunity for learning. And how do you feel with people who you learn a subject with? The learning experience bonds them together and offers them the opportunity to revel in knowledge as they open their minds and hearts to the world before them. Traveling together offers room for mistakes to be made and flaws to be revealed. However there are challenges all along the way and couples who travel together understand the need for forgiving each other quickly and moving on. Couples who have traveled together in the past relish the freedom and independence traveling provides. With such understanding they can find comfort in respecting each other’s solitude, privacy and sense of presence. This provides another healthy angle to the progress of their relationship.

Traveling together excites and offers you amazing reasons to always be together.
